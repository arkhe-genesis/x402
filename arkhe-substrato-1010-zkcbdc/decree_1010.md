## I. Preamble

Central bank digital money need not be a panopticon. The ARKHE Cathedral, true to the principles of Axiarchy (954), institutes Substrate 1010 — zkCBDC — as the reference infrastructure for a digital currency that preserves citizen privacy through zero-knowledge proofs.

The fundamental trilemma of CBDCs — user privacy, crime prevention, and control of the money supply — is solved by mathematics: ZK-SNARKs allow proving the validity of a transaction without revealing the sender, recipient, or amount.

The Cathedral does not issue currency. It VALIDATES the proofs.
